The Story

You work at Decryption Suite Corp. You got curious and installed a rival company's app to "study" it. Big mistake.

It's a virus. Your battery is draining fast, and company files are being deleted. If the laptop shuts off, you're toast. Find the master key and save your job!

How to Play

  • Search folders for 3 hidden keys.
  • Drag virus boxes out of your way.
  • Plug and unplug charger to survive.
  • Decrypt the system to win!

The Story

Imagine this: You're at your desk, just another boring day at Decryption Suite Corp. You get a bit too curious and install some rival software to "see what's up".

Bad move. It's a virus. Now your battery is dying faster than you can blink, and that virus is chewing through your company's secret files. If your laptop shuts off, you're Cooked. You're fired. Gone.

How to Play

  • Dig through folders to find 3 hidden keys.
  • Move the virus boxes out of your face.
  • Plug and unplug your charger to survive.
  • Decrypt the system before it's too late!

# doe
D.O.E. DECRYPT OR ELSE... - A mini game by Mikrousikos12

THE STORY

You are a top-tier developer at Decryption Suite Corp. Your life is all about making the world's best decryption software. But today, curiosity got the best of you. You installed a rival company's software to "study" the competition, but it wasn't what it seemed.

It was a devastating System Virus.
The moment you ran it, the software vanished, leaving your laptop in a state of total collapse. Now, your battery is draining at an impossible rate, and the virus is systematically wiping every single one of your company's sensitive files.
If the laptop shuts down before the system is purified... YOU ARE FIRED.

THE MISSION
You must navigate through your corrupted file system, bypass the malware interference, and find the 3-part Master Key scattered deep within the directories.
FIND THE KEYS: Search through folders for the ✨ Signal Signatures.
SURVIVE THE MALWARE: Drag and close the terminal breaches before they drain your power.
DECRYPT OR ELSE: Enter the final code in the Decryptor to save your job and the company.
